## Notes on figure sourcing

Most figures in this chapter should be diagrams rather than photographs (except where actual biological images like sperm or embryos are essential). Diagrams allow for clear labeling and color-coding of analogous structures.

For the contraceptive table, use current data from CDC or WHO sources (verify failure rates before final publication).

For the Spemann-Mangold experiment, if using a historical diagram, ensure accuracy and cite the original 1924 publication.

The IVF success rate graph should be based on current ASRM (American Society for Reproductive Medicine) data and should include a note about the age variable's dominance over other factors.
